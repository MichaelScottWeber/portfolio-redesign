About Me

Throughout my whole life I’ve been fascinated with making things.

As a kid I used to craft inventions out of broken pens and rubber bands, and later on made my own musical instruments by circuit-bending toys. As an adult I've spent the last ten years working as a professional musician, writing/recording music for TV shows and teaching private and group music lessons.

Once I discovered how challenging and rewarding web development is, though, I haven't looked back. The feeling of creating something out of thin air, something that other people can interact with and find value in, is what drives me to get up every morning and web development offers me that opportunity like nothing else.

As a Front End UI/UX Developer, I am experienced with designing and building user-friendly projects from the ground up and focused on finding new ways to solve everyday problems through technology. I am a fast learner, a hard worker, and a flexible team player who loves attacking new problems head-on.
